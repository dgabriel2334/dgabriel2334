### Engineering philosophy

> Architecture isn't about diagrams — it's about **making change safe and predictable**.

Seven years building systems where downtime costs money, bugs reach thousands of users and billing logic has to stay precise. That shapes how I work: explicit domain boundaries, hexagonal architecture, automated tests as a design tool, and small, reversible changes.
